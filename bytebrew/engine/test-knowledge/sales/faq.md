# Sales FAQ

## What is ByteBrew?
ByteBrew is a universal AI agent engine. Companies add ByteBrew to their product to give it AI capabilities through configuration, not coding.

## What is the pricing?
ByteBrew Community Edition (CE) is completely free with unlimited agents. Enterprise Edition includes horizontal scaling, observability dashboard, and priority support — contact sales for pricing.

## How do I install ByteBrew?
You can install ByteBrew using Docker: `docker compose up -d`. Or download the binary from bytebrew.ai/download.

## What LLM providers are supported?
ByteBrew supports Ollama (local), OpenAI, Anthropic, and any OpenAI-compatible API (like OpenRouter).

## What is the return policy?
ByteBrew CE is free, no purchase needed. For Enterprise Edition, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

## How do I contact support?
Email support@bytebrew.ai or open an issue on GitHub at github.com/syntheticinc/bytebrew.
